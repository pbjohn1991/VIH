About us

V-IH Solutions, LLC is an industrial hygiene consulting firm located in Reisterstown, Maryland. We provide high-quality and cost effective services that meet all regulatory and industry standards. V-IH Solutions offers its services to the state of Maryland; Washington, DC; and Virginia. Our staff consists of qualified and dedicated personnel that possess the skills to perform all work with accuracy and professionalism. We take pride in maintaining positive relationships with our clients and completing projects and surveys on time.
